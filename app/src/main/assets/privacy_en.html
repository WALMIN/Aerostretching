Privacy policy

Valid date: 2020-11-27

Purpose

Anastasia Dobrova (hereinafter referred to as "Aerostretching") has adopted this privacy policy in order to live up to the requirements that current legislation of processing personal data, GDPR.

The purpose of this policy is to inform our customers and other external partners how we process personal data within our company.

Scope

Aerostretching operates aerostretching.se and may operate other application softwares. It is Aerostretching's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our application softwares.

Our Privacy policy describes what personal data we process, with regards to our employees, our customers, our suppliers and our partners. IT also states who the Data Controller is and how to get in contact with the Data Controller. The document also informs how the Data Subject can take advantage of their rights towards us.

Those who work with us, employees, customers, partners and others (we call them registrar) should be confident that their personal data is safe with us. It is based on current personal data protection legislation and clarifies how we should work in order to take advantage of the registrar’s rights and integrity.

Responsibility

The Data Controller is responsible in what way and with what methods personal data should be processed. The Data Controller is also responsible providing the right infrastructure (both tools and methods) to the organisation in order to protect the registrar’s rights.

This task is delegated to the CFO, as the registry manager, to be responsible for GDPR from an operational point of view.

It is every manager’s responsibility to ensure that the personnel have read, understood and work in accordance with the latest version of GDPR procedure.

Application Software Visitors

Like most application software operators, Aerostretching collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Aerostretching's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Aerostretching's visitors use its application software. From time to time, Aerostretching may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its application software.

Aerostretching also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on Aerostretching.com blogs/sites. Aerostretching only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to Aerostretching' apps choose to interact with Aerostretching in ways that require Aerostretching to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Aerostretching gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at Aerostretching.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Aerostretching are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Aerostretching collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with Aerostretching. Aerostretching does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain application software-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

Aerostretching may collect statistics about the behaviour of visitors to its application software’s. Aerostretching may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Aerostretching does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Aerostretching discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Aerostretching' behalf or to provide services available at Aerostretching' apps, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that an application software stores on a visitor's device, and that the visitor's browser provides to the application software each time the visitor returns. Aerostretching uses cookies to help Aerostretching identify and track visitors, their usage of Aerostretching application software, and their application software access preferences. Aerostretching visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their devices should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Aerostretching' apps, with the drawback that certain features of Aerostretching' apps may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Business Transfers

If Aerostretching, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Aerostretching goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Aerostretching may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Ads

Ads appearing on any of our apps may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your device each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your device. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Aerostretching and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
